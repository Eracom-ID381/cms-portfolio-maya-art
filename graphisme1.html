K INSPO

MAYA STALLA, 2020

Quel est le propos de ce travail ?

Comme vous le voyez, je suis très inspirée par le graphisme des magazines de mode.Et plus particulièrement, l'esthétique coréenne qui est présente dans la Kpop.

Donc pour ce travail, j'ai décidé d'explorer ma propore façon de concevoir cette ethétique avec un point de vue péus occidental.

What is this work about ?

As you can see, I am very inspired by fashion magazines design. But more specifically the soft Korean aesthetics which is frequently seen in Kpop.

So for this work, I decided to explore my own way of doing it with my western point of view.

1)Event Poster

First version

For that first one I wanted to keep the visual Identity of CHUU as well as the vibe of the brand.

After deciding that I wanted to do a canvas of what an event advertisement would be based on this aesthetic, I thought I should add something to make it more fun.

Second version

In here you can see that I added some party elements as if it were even more casual and friendly. But I would say that the upside down letter are no more necessary in this version.
